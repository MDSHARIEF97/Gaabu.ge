Welcome to GAABU

GAABU plays a pivotal role in commercial cooperation in Georgia, and GAABU’s membership is made up of companies of various types and sizes: large companies, small and medium enterprises. and private companies, as well as organizations and individual businessmen.

The Georgian Arabic African Business Union

The board structure is designed to be the primary source of support that provides both; International and Georgian business communities, with a wide range of services and support functions. To overcome problems and enhance job opportunities that would not be possible for individuals.

Vision

  • To promote trade and industrial development
  • To enhance business opportunities
  • To serve all of the companies to achieve their success in business wise;
  • To encourage and increase the volume of tourism between the countries;

Mission

  • The most valued service provider for businesses engaged in bilateral trade, service providers and investors
  • To be the representative body for Arabic and African business in Georgia
  • Accepted dialogue partner for all Arabic, African and Georgian institutions.
Meet our Founder
With more than 12 years of international experience in equity management, Mr. Habashi has been doing business in Egypt since 2008 in the field of Airport supply. In 2011, he launched his business in the field of social responsibility in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, In 2013, he started a new business called Social Entrepreneur Consultants that promoted the idea of benefiting the community when doing business. Mr. Hebashi has developed a consulting and investment firm in Malaysia with a focus on real estate. Apart from the real estate business, Mr. Habashi is the Chairman of the Board of Directors of the Egyptian Chamber of Commerce in Georgia, the founder and CEO of Tiflis Arabic Media TV.
